Cladding

Natural veneer cladded walls are amongst the most popular of any stone features. They bring so much life to any project creating that warm and homely feel to your property. Cladding also breathes life into those darker corners of the room with a heart-warming fireplace wrapped in stone or bright stone fetcher wall.

There are many reasons for using sawn back thin stone cladding. It can not only create an outstanding look of solid structurally built stone walls without taking up too much room, but also there is that added beauty and benefit where you can bring any of your most favourite places from around the world and have it come to life in your very own backyard or living room.

Whether it is that gorgeous Greek look, that bespoke Balinese feeling or that elegant English style, we can bring that dream to life.

Cladding can significantly reduce the weight on a specific area compared to solid stone walls.

Crazy paving

Crazy paving is ideal for those outside finishing touches or creating that floorless stone feeling flowing through the property. You can elegantly add crazy paving to any area as it blends beautifully with any design. It is also very resilient.

Crazy paving is a fantastic finish to have almost anywhere in or outside your home: from courtyards, to pool sides, through bedrooms to your balcony, even your driveways and not to forget your front entrance to your forever home.

Solid stone walls

Solid stone is perfect for retaining walls. It also doubles as a stunning feature. These days we can see that solid stone is gaining more popularity mainly because of the practical reasons like acting as a great natural insulation for any build and being extremely tough as well as having a timeless look and being locally sourced from sustainable quarries.

There are two different types of solid stone walling so you can pick your fancy. You could pick a solid dry stack for that incredible finish or grout which gives it a bespoke character and heritage look.

We create freestanding and retaining walls from ground up. This is solid structurally laid stone as well as veneer tied into existing or new block work.

Mechanical Fixings

At The Gentleman’s Stone we can install hard and heavy panels of stone from mechanical aid for façade, substructure or substrate. This can be done from engineered clips that are best described as being a dry fixed bracket, secured by either a bolt to steel work, screw bolt or through bolt to concrete or masonry substrate, Pan headed S S wood screw onto plywood or compressed sheeting and then there is no reliance on the epoxy or glue. Most mechanical aid is used to hide services to

Create that stunning feature but also used to comply with building regulations for veneer stone walls over a certain height.
